About Us

At WEBI, we not only aim to solve problems with clever software but also with an attitude of responsibility and service to the customers and communities we engage with. As we build our teams, we seek out technical experts guided by principles of honesty, perseverance and reliability. In an expression of our commitment to positive social impact, our team members regularly volunteer and serve with both national and local non-profit organizations, in addition to founding our own non-profit social empowerment projects.
